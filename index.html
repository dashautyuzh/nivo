Intro

Nivo

(nē¦vō) noun
Niveau is the french word for level.

Nivo is a cloud-based tool that serves as a virtual blueprint for websites. It would visually represnt how a site's pages/URIs are structured using interconnected tiles and page-specific information. The structure and individual pages' elements would be exportable to allow for edits outside the platform.
Why Nivo?
Our existing internal processes are often cumbersome across disciplines, relying on many Word and Excel-based deliverables that change hands frequently, undergo multiple iterations and can be lost when team members leave or go on vacation. The construction and maintenance of these files takes away from time that could be spent adding more value to our projects or winning additional work. Nivo would make us more efficient and improve our delivery for clients, potentially making us a more attractive option compared to competitors.
Our Goal
Our primary goal is to create efficiencies in existing processes so that our agency can deliver better work faster while improving the margin on existing client engagements. We also seek to further solidify our position as Educators and Innovators both through the work we do, and through how we deliver it.
